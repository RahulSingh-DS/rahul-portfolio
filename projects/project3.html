Problem Statement

Retail businesses need to understand their customer base to run targeted marketing campaigns. This project segments mall customers based on their annual income and spending score to identify actionable customer groups for personalized strategies.

Approach & Methodology

01

Exploratory Data Analysis

Analyzed 200+ customer records — distribution of age, income, and spending patterns. Identified outliers and performed normalization.

02

Data Preprocessing

Applied normalization and outlier handling to ensure clean feature space for clustering.

03

Optimal K Selection

Used the Elbow Method and Silhouette Score to determine the ideal number of clusters, landing on k=5.

04

Cluster Visualization & Interpretation

Visualized clusters using Matplotlib scatter plots and labeled each segment with actionable business meaning.
