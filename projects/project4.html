Problem Statement

Retail businesses need accurate sales forecasts to optimize inventory, staffing, and marketing spend. This project builds and compares two time series forecasting models — ARIMA and Prophet — to predict future sales trends across multiple product datasets.

Approach & Methodology

01

Time Series Decomposition

Analyzed trend, seasonality, and residual components to understand the underlying patterns in sales data.

02

ARIMA Modeling

Fitted ARIMA model with optimal (p,d,q) parameters using ACF/PACF plots and AIC/BIC criterion for parameter selection.

03

Prophet Modeling

Used Facebook's Prophet for automatic seasonality detection and holiday effects — more robust for business datasets.

04

Visualization & Pipeline

Built actual vs predicted charts using Matplotlib and Plotly for stakeholder-friendly reporting. Wrapped everything in a reusable pipeline for multiple datasets.
